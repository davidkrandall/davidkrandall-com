Product

Powerplay

Product

Powerplay is application designed to help friends and neighbors better understand their energy consumption and find ways to save through analysis and competition. The project was completed by a team of four Masters in Human-Computer Interaction students at Carnegie Mellon. See a poster summarizing of all key features and insights here.
